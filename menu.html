Swedish Cardamom Buns

Willow Bakery's award winning Swedish cardamom buns are the first pastry to sell out each day. This sweet knotted pastry imbued with the southern indian spice and coated in light layers of butter is the best way to start anyone's day. $4.25

Variety of Cupcakes

Willow Bakery has a variety of cupcake and icing combinations daily. Possible cupcake flavors include vanilla, chocolate, red velvet, lemon blueberry, carrot, and pumpkin. Possible buttercream frosting flavors include chocolate, vanilla, mocha, nutella, raspberry, lemon, and maple. $1.75

Croissants

A buttery, flaky, layered pastry made fresh in our oven daily. These croissants are delicious as they are paired with morning coffee or tea, but can also be used in a variety of creative ways for every meal. $2.25

Variety of Doughnuts

Our display cases each morning hold a variety of freshly baked doughnuts, available in the following flavors: original glazed, chocolate glazed, old-fashioned, maple iced, apple tart, blueberry, strawberry, lemon, or rainbow sprinkled. $2.25

Variety of Macarons

Last but not least, our bakery offers various flavors of macarons, randomized each day. Possible flavors include: chocolate mint, matcha green tea, vanilla, strawberry, cookies and cream, espresso, coconut, red velvet, pistachio, or cookie dough. Come in or call to see which flavors we're offering for the day! $1.75
